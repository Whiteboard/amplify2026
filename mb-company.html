Our team

Who we are

Technology for missional organizations was falling short. So we raised the bar. Ministry Brands has helped over 90,000 organizations grow and amplify their impact on the world.

We provide community and organizational leaders with integrated solutions covering people and donor management, digital giving, websites, communications, media, event planning, background screenings, and more. Together, our nearly 700 associates help clients transform how they operate and create real, positive change for the world we live in.

Purpose

We leverage technology for your mission

The world of technology is our element.

Our years of experience have taught us clever ways to streamline processes, strengthen connections, and create secure environments for generosity. But we’ll never forget that on both sides of our technology are humans — working together toward a common goal: your mission.

Our element

Tech that streamlines, connects, and protects

Years of working alongside missional organizations have taught us how to clear the friction out of process, connection, and security — without losing the warmth of the work itself.

People, not platforms

Humans on both sides of every screen

It would be easy to focus on dashboards and integrations. We never forget that on both sides of our technology are real people — volunteers, pastors, donors, our own team — working toward something that matters.

Your mission, our purpose

You’re the expert in your mission

We’re the experts in helping you reach it. That distinction shapes every product decision, every support call, and every line of code — and it’s the reason 90,000+ organizations trust us with theirs.

Two-tier purpose: Live `/about` uses a single dense prose block for the purpose statement. We keep the original copy as the lead paragraph but follow it with three bite-sized principles. Lets a skimmer get the gist without reading the prose, while preserving the original voice for those who do.
