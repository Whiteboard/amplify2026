Common questions
Generosity Bundle FAQ
What's included in the Generosity Bundle?
+
The Generosity Bundle includes four products: Giving, Accounting, Mobile App, and Communications. Together, they cover online and mobile giving, fund accounting and reconciliation, a branded church app, and donor communication tools including campaigns, thank-you messages, and year-end statements.
Can I add more products later?
+
Yes, you can add any Amplify product to your bundle at any time. Many churches start with the Generosity Bundle and later add People (ChMS), Streaming, or other tools as their needs grow. Your account team can help you plan the right path.
Is there a cost savings vs buying individually?
+
Yes, bundles are priced below the combined cost of purchasing each product separately. You also get bundle-exclusive features like auto-sync between Giving and Accounting, consolidated reporting, and dedicated bundle support that aren't available with individual product purchases.
How long does setup take?
+
Most churches are fully live within 2–4 weeks. Our implementation team handles data migration, platform configuration, and staff training. We coordinate the rollout so all four products launch together seamlessly, with no disruption to your existing giving.
Can I migrate from another giving platform?
+
Absolutely. Our team handles data migration from your current giving provider, including donor records, recurring gift schedules, and historical giving data. We ensure recurring gifts continue without interruption so you never miss a donation during the transition.
