Frequently asked
Questions Before Your Demo
How long is the demo? +
Most demos run about 30 minutes. We’ll focus on the areas most relevant to your church, so you get real answers — not a generic product tour.
Is there any obligation or cost? +
None. The demo is completely free with no strings attached. We want you to see if Amplify is the right fit before making any decisions.
Who will I be talking to? +
A church technology specialist who works with ministries like yours every day. They understand the challenges of running a church, not just the software features.
Can I bring my team? +
Absolutely. In fact, we encourage it. Having your key decision-makers on the call helps us tailor the demo and answer everyone’s questions at once.
What if I’m already using another platform? +
That’s very common. We can show you how Amplify compares and walk you through what a migration would look like. Our team has handled thousands of transitions with zero data loss.
Can I see specific products during the demo? +
Yes — that’s the point. When you submit the form, tell us which areas you’re most interested in and we’ll build the demo around those. Giving, streaming, ChMS, or the full platform — it’s your call.
