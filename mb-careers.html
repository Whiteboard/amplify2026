Why we do this work

We work for the people who serve people.

Every day, the churches, parishes, and nonprofits we serve do the hard work of holding communities together. Our team shows up for them — building software that takes the friction out of ministry so they can spend more time on what matters.

Our Commitment

Building a workplace as inclusive as the communities we serve

We are committed to a workplace where every team member is valued, every voice is heard, and every background is welcomed. Our diversity and inclusion work is a year-round practice — not a campaign — and includes our active partnership with the National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI).
