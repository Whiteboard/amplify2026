One Family, Many Solutions
Forty-plus years of building for mission

Ministry Brands started with a simple idea: that the software supporting churches, parishes, and nonprofits should be as committed to mission as the organizations it serves.

Today, four purpose-built platforms work together under one parent — each engineered for the unique workflows and people of its category, while sharing a foundation of security, support, and stewardship.
