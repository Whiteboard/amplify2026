One Database. Shared Across Every Tool.
When a member gives on Sunday, checks in their child, and RSVPs for a small group — all of that lives in one place. Contacts, giving history, attendance, and communications all flow between products automatically. No CSV exports, no manual reconciliation, no data gaps.
