A week in your life with Spired for Churches
From Monday Planning to Sunday Morning Peace
Here's what it looks like when your worship tools actually work together. No frantic group texts. No missing chord charts. Just a team that shows up prepared.
Worship Service Plan
Songs, cues, volunteers, and media in one view
Monday 9:00 AM — Build the Plan
Build this Sunday's service order with songs, readings, and cues
Open the worship flow editor, drop in songs from your library, add scripture readings, and set transition cues. The whole service takes shape in minutes.
✓ Service Planning
Wednesday 11:00 AM — Confirm the Team
Confirm volunteer team — auto-reminders sent, see who accepted
Automated reminders went out Monday. By Wednesday you see green checkmarks next to every role — vocalists, sound tech, projector operator. One slot open? Send a targeted request.
@ Communications + ✓ Service Planning
Thursday 3:00 PM — Finalize Arrangements
Finalize song arrangements, attach chord charts and tracks
Attach chord charts, lyric sheets, and backing tracks directly to each song in the service plan. Your band can pull them up on their phones at rehearsal.
✓ Service Planning + ◉ Media
Saturday 10:00 AM — Tech Rehearsal
Run tech rehearsal, live stream test
Walk through the service plan with your tech team. Test the live stream, check audio levels, and confirm every media cue is loaded. No surprises tomorrow.
▶ Streaming + ◉ Media
Sunday 7:00 AM — Ready to Lead
Open the service plan on your phone — everything is there, everyone is confirmed
Pull up the plan on your phone. Every song, every cue, every volunteer — confirmed and ready. Walk into the building knowing Sunday is handled.
