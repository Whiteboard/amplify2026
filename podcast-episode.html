About This Episode

In this episode, we sit down with Toby Walters, founder of ChurchGear, to explore the delicate balance between embracing modern church technology and preserving the sacred, reverent atmosphere that makes a worship space feel set apart.

Toby shares lessons learned from equipping hundreds of churches with audio, video, and streaming systems — and why the best tech implementations are the ones the congregation never notices. From LED walls to in-ear monitors, he breaks down how to make smart investments that serve the mission without overwhelming the moment.

Whether you are a worship pastor navigating your first major AV upgrade or an executive pastor trying to justify the budget, this conversation will help you think more clearly about where technology fits in your ministry's story.

Discussion Topics

  • Why "invisible technology" is the gold standard for church AV
  • How to evaluate streaming platforms and what most churches get wrong
  • Building a volunteer-friendly tech booth that doesn't require an engineer
  • The ROI conversation: helping your board see tech as ministry investment
  • Balancing acoustic worship with amplified environments
  • Common mistakes churches make when upgrading their media setup
