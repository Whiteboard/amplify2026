Our Story in Galway
Built in Ireland, shipping worldwide

Ministry Brands opened its Galway centre in 2019, drawn by Ireland’s deep talent pool in SaaS engineering, product design, and customer-facing roles. What started as a small platform team has grown into a multi-discipline hub of more than fifty people.

From Galway we build core pieces of our church, parish, and nonprofit platforms — including the giving infrastructure that processes hundreds of millions of dollars in donations each year and the data services that connect our four brands.

Our CTO is based here. So is platform engineering, a growing product and design team, and a site reliability function that keeps services running for customers in every US time zone and across Europe.
